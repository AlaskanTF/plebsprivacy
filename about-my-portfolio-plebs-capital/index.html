1. Fund Overview & Philosophy

The Story

Plebs Capital was founded in 2022 out of a passion for financial markets and a belief that  investing should be accessible to everyone not just the rich, the accredited or institutional investors.

My journey began in 2016, when I first entered the markets and lost nearly 90% of my savings due to being fucking dumb. That failure became my greatest teacher. After earning my B.A. in Economics and actively studying and understanding how markets truly work, I decided to put that knowledge into practice.

Starting with limited capital and the support of family and friends who believed in me, I started this portfolio, a fund built by and for everyday people (plebs). Managing money for others brought a new level of responsibility and discipline. Every decision I make is guided by one principle: to act in the best interest of the fund and its investors, focusing on sustainable, risk-managed growth.

Mission Statement:
Our mission is to empower everyday investors through disciplined, transparent, and performance-driven investing.

2. Investment Strategy

The portfolio is 60% visionary and 40% opportunistic focused primarily on equities and options. This blend allows the fund to pursue long-term, high-conviction investments while maintaining tactical flexibility to capture near-term opportunities and manage risk while generating income.

Visionary Strategy (60%)

The visionary portion targets companies that we believe will be needed and increasingly needed in the future. We also analyse their supply chains, as growth in the primary company often drives growth in suppliers. For example, we identified NVIDIA (NVDA) at around $19 per share, recognizing that GPUs initially for gaming would become critical to AI as we saw their computing capabilities it had for mining Bitcoin. Anticipating NVIDIA’s expansion, we invested in key suppliers such as Micron (MU) and TSMC, capturing upside across the value chain.

Opportunistic Strategy (40%)

The opportunistic allocation focuses on shorter-term, valuation-driven opportunities, primarily using options:

  • Waiting for favourable entry points when valuations are high.

  • Selling puts to generate income or acquire stock at attractive prices.

  • Selling covered calls on profitable holdings to lock in gains while managing risk.

This hybrid structure allows the portfolio to remain adaptable while continuously compounding value for investors.

3. Track Record & Performance

Since inception in 2022, the portfolio has a total return of approximately 383%. The 2025 year-to-date return is currently 63.26%. ( as of 10 November 2025)

Key Drivers of Performance

Returns have been driven by concentrated, high-conviction equity investments and tactical options strategies with no ETFs or passive funds.

Volatility and Vision

The fund has experienced drawdowns of roughly 25%, reflecting the nature of visionary investing. Temporary declines are viewed as part of the long-term growth process.

Performance Mindset

Beyond outperforming the market, the fund seeks to beat its own previous results, fostering continuous improvement and disciplined decision-making.

5. Risk Management

Diversification and Position Sizing

The fund maintains sector diversification and limits individual positions to generally below 15% of portfolio value, balancing conviction with risk control.

Hedging and Market Protection

Protective puts are used to hedge against identified market-wide risks.

Liquidity and Cash Reserves

Holding 10–30% of the portfolio in cash according to market sentiment allows flexibility to capitalize on opportunities and mitigate downside during market downturns.

6. Future Outlook & Strategy

For 2026 and beyond, the fund focuses on identifying companies at risk of being displaced and the innovators replacing them. The investment strategy remains unchanged, as it scales effectively with growing AUM.

While market direction cannot be predicted, the fund prioritizes adaptability, research, and conviction, aiming to outperform both the market and its own previous results.
